How the Search Works

The Scopus Search by FF allows you to search for academic articles and papers from the Scopus database. To perform a search, follow these steps:

  1. Enter your search query in the "What to search" field. You can use keywords or phrases relevant to your topic.
  2. Select the search scope from the "Search in" dropdown menu. You can choose to search in the title, abstract, or both.
  3. Click the "Search on Scopus" button.

The search supports complex queries using logical operators. For example, you can use parentheses and logical operators to create complex queries like: (cyber OR security) AND (bias OR perception). This query will find articles that contain either "cyber" or "security" in their titles or abstracts, and also contain either "bias" or "perception".
